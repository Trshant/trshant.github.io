Coconut dry masala

1.5 Cup Grated or Desicated Coconut 2.5 tsp Coriander Powder 1.5 tsp Cumin Powder 0.25 tsp Turmeric Powder 0.25 tsp + 0.125 tsp Salt 0.25 tsp Sugar 0.125 tsp Hing ( Asefoiteda ) 0.25 tsp Chilli powder

Mix all and store. If using dessicated can store in cool and dry place. If using grated coconut, mix needs to be stored in the fridge.

Use with puffed rice to make snack

Add 1 measure of Masala mix with 2 measures of Puffed rice. Add 2 tsp of any oil. Add finely chopped onion for a much tastier snack.

Stuffed Baby Okra

Make a slit and stuff the masala in the Okra. Heat oil and put the stuffed Okra in it. Take care while turning the okra over. will take about 40 minutes (or till the okra is cooked ) over a low-medium flame.
