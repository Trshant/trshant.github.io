Chopsuey

For the curry

Add these to a vessal: 1 cup broth 2 tbsp soy 2 tbsp sweet chilli 3 tbsp ketchup 1 breast shredded chicken boil it for 20 minutes with a lid on. 2 tbsp flour for thickening 1.5 tbsp vinegar at end

for the fried noodles:

Cook 2 noodles. drain water. Heat enough oil in the frying pan. Fry the noodles, Each part is a serving, so decide accordingly

The dish itself

Keep the noodles itself and on a dish on the side keep the curry. This is a simple, but expensive looking meal.
