From the time i programmed this blog i wondered how to change the title of the page to display the title i wanted to show the page by, but i always thought it would have something to do with services. However after a few evenings spent reading the manual did nothing to address this issue. However, today i just thought that maybe we can do the whole thing using JavaScript.

I was under the impression that i needed to add a change_the_title function as the object returned by one of the services. However it was stupidly simple.

document.title = post.title

Yes, thats what i had to add to make at the right place to do the needful.

Moral of the story: Think Simple, Stupid!
